# How It Works — Stock Image Placeholders

Replace each file below with a high-quality stock photo (landscape, ~1200x900px minimum).

## Required images

### answer-triage.jpg
A calm professional answering a phone at a workstation during a night shift.
Warm lighting, muted tones. Think: focused care worker with a headset in a
quiet office environment.

### resolve-escalate.jpg
Team coordination and escalation. A small team reviewing information on a screen
or whiteboard — conveying structured communication. Think: two people at a desk
collaborating with soft, professional lighting.

### document-improve.jpg
Reporting, compliance, and documentation. A clean dashboard or someone reviewing
printed reports at a desk. Think: organised paperwork or a laptop showing a
summary report, professional and tidy.

## Notes
- Use royalty-free stock photos (Unsplash, Pexels, Shutterstock, etc.)
- Keep aspect ratio close to 4:3 for best fit in the layout.
- If an image file is missing, the component renders a neutral gradient
  fallback automatically — the layout will not break.
